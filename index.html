Our Mission

Projet 'Endless Space' one of the 'Starship Interprise' company's projects. The mission of this project is to help people see and feel the cosmos.

Our Journeys

Journey into orbit

A trip to orbit is a starting journey that allows you to look at the planet Earth in all its glory and feel the infinity of the universe. Recommended for the first journey

Journey to the Moon

A trip to the moon allows you to walk on thier surface and collect several samples for research. Recommended for the second journey

Journey to the Venus

A trip to the Venus allows you to walk on their surface with speshial ship, see the perfect views. Recommended for the third journey

Journey to the Mars

A trip to the Mars allows you to to walk on their surface, see the perfect views and try to grow up the potatoues in the speshial boxes. Recommended for the fourth journey
